Hello! My name is Hagen Zhang,

and here's what that means to me:


I was born in May of 2002, and grew up in the California Bay Area. My parents are both Chinese, and I have one older brother who is 8 years older than me. I go by he/him pronouns.

I'm a student at Northeastern Univserity, studying for a B.S. in Computer Science with a concentration in Software Development.
My current plan is to graduate in 2024, which is coming up a lot sooner than I admittedly feel ready for!

Alongside with being a programmer, I'm also very into music and the outdoors!
I'm a mostly self-taught musician, and my primary instrument at the moment is the electric guitar (although I also do know how to play drums, bass, and a tiny bit of piano)

I took piano lessons and studied classical music for a few years in elementary school, but then decided to drop it in favor of learning how to play the drum kit. I had an amazing drum teacher who taught me for 5 years and helped me find meaning and genuine enjoyment in music making, rather than just playing the notes on a sheet of paper.

I played drums for various church worship teams until my third year of highschool, when I decided to also pick up the guitar since there was a lack of guitarists at my home church. I eventually fell in love with the instrument, and now I treat the guitar as my primary instrument! Wild how life works out.

My love for the outdoors stems from my background in the Boy Scouts. I was a part of Troop 485 for 4 years, and during that time I had the privilege of being able to experience campouts, hikes, and backpacking trips on a monthly basis. I've completed High Adventures like Northern Tier and Sea Base, and also had the chance to server as a the Troop Leader (also known as the SPL) for a term.
